leinster house was the former duke residence in dublin of the duke of leinster , and since 1922 served as the parliament building of the irish free state , the predecessor of the modern irish state , before which it functioned as the headquarters of the royal dublin society the building is the meeting place of dáil éireann and seanad éireann , the two houses of the oireachtas , and as such the term 'leinster house ' has become a metonym for irish political activities its medieval parliament consisted of two houses , a house of commons and a house of lords ( for the rest of the year , they
